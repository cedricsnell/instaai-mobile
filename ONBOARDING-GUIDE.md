## Troubleshooting

### Can't Connect Instagram

**Problem:** OAuth connection fails

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure Instagram is connected to Facebook Business
2. Check you're admin of the Facebook Page
3. Clear browser cache and try again
4. Use same device for Facebook and Instagram login
5. Verify Instagram is set to Business/Creator mode

### Sync Fails

**Problem:** Content library won't sync

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Ensure Instagram account has posts (min 5 recommended)
3. Verify OAuth permissions weren't revoked
4. Wait 5 minutes and try again
5. Log out and log back in

### Content Generation Issues

**Problem:** AI fails to generate ideas

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure you have at least 10 posts synced
2. Check posts have engagement data
3. Wait for sync to fully complete
4. Try generating fewer ideas (5 instead of 10)
5. Check API key is configured (contact support)

### Scheduling Doesn't Work

**Problem:** Posts not publishing at scheduled time

**Solutions:**
1. Verify Instagram permissions include "publish"
2. Check scheduled time is in the future
3. Ensure account is still connected
4. Check post meets Instagram requirements (format, size, etc.)
5. Review failed post error message for details
